Significance

We got your Back.

From being in high school for almost 4 years now with research supporting it, we can confidently say that many students have bad posture. Now, this is not their fault. The shape of the backpacks they wear with all those heavy textbooks and binders are the main culprit for encouraging bad posture. Despite there being an existing alternative, such as educating children to wear their backpacks properly, it is simply not practical as it would require much of the schoolâ€™s resources, time, and money to execute; which is not ideal.


It is also obvious that many high school students stress out for tests, presentations, projects, etc. We wanted to take out 'two birds with one stone' with the creation of the Backage. This is possible because many sources link bad posture to poor mental health, references can be found below. It not only solves both of the problems, but it exceeds its expectations. The quality and price of the backpack is sure to be successful and helpful for generations to come.

The Backage is sure to be a success and game changer for many students to come. It comes in at a low price, affordable to students, comfortable and minimalistic design, fixes posture and back/neck pain, as well as helps deal with stressful school life. We got your back.
