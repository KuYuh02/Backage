Anatomy of the Product

We got your Back.

The following Image on the left is a prototype of our product, the backage. The name comes from the the two words "Back" and "Package". This product aims to not only fix you back posture which comes with numerous benefits including better mental health.



We have modified the original backpack straps in a way that encourages better posture. This is by tightening the shoulders so that they are not hunched in front of you so that the top half of your body is straight. It also has an additional strap near the bottom that wraps around the back and abdomen to encourage a straight backbone so that the bottom half of your body is also straight.



In addition to the straps, it also comes with a set a standarized pockets and compartments like a regular backpack, enought to fit your daily belongings such as binders, laptops, notebooks, etc. These are the main mechanisms in the backage, customers can also choose to include water repellent material, locked zippers, and insulated lunch compartments.
